Welcome to Exam Paper Management System

Introducing our web application designed to manage periodic test question papers for different departments. With separate databases for each department, faculty members can easily upload and re-upload their question papers for approval by the Head of Department. Once approved, the question papers are sent for printing. Our user-friendly interface streamlines the process, making it faster and more convenient for everyone involved.

Learn More

Users

Faculty

Faculty members are responsible for creating and submitting question papers for their respective courses. With our system, they can track the status of their submissions.

Login
HOD

The HOD is responsible for reviewing the question papers submitted by faculty members in their department. They have the power to approve or disapprove question papers.

Login
Examination Cell

The examination cell is responsible for printing the approved question papers and distributing them to students. With our system, they have access to all the approved question papers from all the departments.

Login

Features

Secure Login

All users (faculty, hod, and examination cell) can securely log in with their respective roles and permissions to access the system.

User-Friendly Interface

Faculty can easily upload and manage question papers, track their status, and re-upload if necessary.

Approval System

HOD can approve or disapprove submitted question papers, with the ability to provide reasons for disapproval.

Direct Integration

Approved question papers are directly integrated with the examination cell for easy printing.

DBMS

HOD can manage faculty login credentials, register new faculty, and update data based on course name or code.

Robust Security Measures

The system is equipped with robust security measures to protect sensitive information and prevent unauthorized access.

Time-saving

The system saves time for the examination cell as they do not need to manually collect question papers from each department.

Printing

System ensures that only approved question papers are sent to the examination cell for printing.

University Limitation

The system is limited to use within a college or university and cannot be used outside the institution.
