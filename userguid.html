User Guide

Getting Started

Here are the steps to get started with our exam paper management system:

  1. Select the role you belogs to from navigation links.
  2. Click it , and enter the login credentials.
  3. Use login credentials provided to you.

Faculty

Here are some tips for Faculty:

  • You can login with your login credentials.
  • You will see the question papers submitted by you.
  • If you didnt submitted any question paper, you can submit it by clicking on Upload a question paper button.
  • You can ddelete or reupload a question paper.
  • Once you have submitted a question paper HOD can review it.
  • HOD can approve or disapprove your question paper, if it is disapproved, hod can give you a reason for that, Based on that reason you can resubmit question paper.
  • If hod approved your question paper it will be sent to examination cell for printing.
  • You can see the status of your question paper in your page.
  • You cant delete or reupload a question paper if they are approved by HOD.
  • You can delete or reupload a question paper if they are disapproved by HOD or until the HOD didnt take any action, means till the status is defaut(-).

HOD

Here are some tips for HOD:

  • You can login with your login credentials.
  • You will see the question papers submitted by faculty in your page.
  • You can approve or disapprove the question paper submitted by you department facultys.
  • You have to give a reason if you disapproving question paper.
  • Once you disapproved a question paper, faculty can resubmit the que paper and you can check it again.
  • If you approved a question paper, and now you want to disapprove it you can do that also and the question paper is being removed from examination cell also.
  • If you disapprove a question paper and later you want to approve it before faculty can reupload or delete it you can do that also, and the reason you provided will be changed to (-).
  • You can change the name for Head Of Department.
  • You can register a new faculty(Login Credentials).
  • You can update a faculty data based on either course name or course code or both.
  • You can see the all faculty details in Delete Faculty Page, and you can delete a particular faculty login credentials through that page.
  • Once you deleted the faculty detials, that faculty would no longer be able to login to the system.

Examination Cell

Here are some tips for HOD:

  • You can login with your login credentials.
  • You will see the all question papers approved by HOD's.
  • You can see Faculty name , branch , course name, course code and other necessary information.
  • You can directly print the question papers by clicking on print button appearing next to each aproved question paper record.
  • If you approved a question paper, and now you want to disapprove it you can do that also and the question paper is being removed from examination cell also.
